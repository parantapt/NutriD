About us

NutriDOME is easy to use, affordable, portable, and most importantly, educational. It consists of a properly portioned bowl, divided into the five main food groups, where students will sort foods into, in order to practice and grow their knowledge. With NutriDOME implementation in schools, children everywhere will be able to start and maintain a healthy diet that they can continue throughout their lives.

EAccording to statistics from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, a total of 78 million adults and 12.5 million adolescents are obese in the United States[1]. To prevent a lifestyle of unhealthy eating habits and a poor diet, nutrition needs to be taught to children at a young age, particularly in schools.

When confronted with the challenge to design a 3D printed learning activity to implement in schools, our team decided to confront this task of nutrition education. Specifically, we aim to educate elementary students, so that they can develop a firm grasp of nutrition health early on in their life.
